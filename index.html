Edgar Brea

Mixing data science and innovation management to help unleash the power of human creativity.


Industry Research Fellow @ University of Queensland

I have significant research and consulting experience using data science and rigorous research methods to discover insights and support strategic decisions around innovation. I have assessed the value of emerging technologies, identified opportunities for growth, designed data-driven methods to generate business insights, and investigated optimal ways for organisations to innovate and adapt to change.

I have designed, conducted and managed a number of research projects involving a variety of stakeholders across different industries from biotechnology and agribusiness to energy and banking. To achieve this, I have complemented by background in information systems with formal research training, and theoretical and practical knowledge in business and management.

I have a Bachelor of Computer Systems Engineering from Universidad Metropolitana (Caracas, Venezuela), a Master of Technology and Innovation Management, and a PhD in innovation management, both from the University of Queensland (Brisbane, Australia).
